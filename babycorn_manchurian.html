Baby Corn Manchurian Recipe

Ingredients

  • For Baby Corn Manchurian:
    • 10-12 baby corn, cut into bite-sized pieces
    • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons cornflour
    • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
    • 1 teaspoon red chili sauce
    • 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper powder
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for deep frying
  • For Manchurian Sauce:
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1 bell pepper (capsicum), chopped
    • 2-3 green chilies, sliced
    • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon tomato ketchup
    • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon vinegar
    • Salt to taste
    • Spring onions, chopped (for garnishing)

Instructions

  1. For Baby Corn Manchurian:
    • Make a batter with all-purpose flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, soy sauce, red chili sauce, vinegar, black pepper powder, and salt.
    • Dip baby corn pieces in the batter and deep fry until golden and crispy.
  2. For Manchurian Sauce:
    • Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions, bell pepper, and green chilies. Saut√© until vegetables are slightly cooked.
    • Add soy sauce, tomato ketchup, sugar, vinegar, and salt. Mix well.
    • Add fried baby corn pieces and toss them in the sauce until well coated.
    • Garnish with chopped spring onions.
