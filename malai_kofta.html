Malai Kofta Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • For Koftas:
    • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
    • 1/2 cup crumbled paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
    • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, etc.)
    • 2 tablespoons chopped raisins
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 2 tablespoons cornflour
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for frying
  • For Gravy:
    • 1 cup tomato puree
    • 1/2 cup onion paste
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1/4 cup cashew paste
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1/4 cup fresh cream
    • 2 tablespoons butter
    • Oil for cooking
    • Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the koftas by mixing boiled potatoes, crumbled paneer, chopped nuts, raisins, garam masala, red chili powder, cornflour, and salt.
  2. Shape the mixture into balls and deep fry until golden brown.
  3. For the gravy, heat oil and butter in a pan. Add onion paste and sauté until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add cashew paste, garam masala, and fresh cream. Mix well.
  7. Assemble the koftas and gravy before serving.
