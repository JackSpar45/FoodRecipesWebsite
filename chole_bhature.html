Chole Bhature Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • For Chole (Chickpea Curry):
    • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight and boiled)
    • 2 onions, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, pureed
    • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for cooking
  • For Bhature (Fried Bread):
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
    • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • 1 tablespoon oil
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for deep frying

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Prepare the chole (chickpea curry) by heating oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute until the raw smell disappears.
  4. Add tomato puree, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook the masala until oil starts to separate.
  5. Add boiled chickpeas and garam masala. Mix well and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  6. For the bhature, mix all-purpose flour, semolina, baking powder, baking soda, yogurt, oil, and salt. Knead into a soft dough. Let it rest for 2 hours.
  7. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into round discs.
  8. Heat oil for deep frying. Fry the bhature until they puff up and turn golden brown.
  9. Serve hot bhature with the chole curry.
