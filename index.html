Famous Indian Food Recipes

Pav Bhaji

Pav bhaji are spiced mashed vegetables served in soft bread rolls. They are a cornerstone of street food in Mumbai and made with their own masala spice blend.

Hyderabadi Biriyani

Hyderabadi biryani (also known as Hyderabadi dum biryani) is a style of biryani originating from kitchens of the Nizam of Hyderabad, made with basmati rice, goat, mutton, or chicken meat, yogurt, onions and saffron.

Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy curry made with paneer, spices, onions, tomatoes, cashews and butter. It is cooked in butter which imparts its characteristic buttery flavor to the dish.

Babycorn Manchurian is deep fried baby corn dumplings coated with a crispy spicy layer and tossed in chinese sauces. It is cooked with chinese seasonings and cooking techniques to appeal Indian taste buds.

Mysore Masala Dosa

Mysore Masala Dosa is a popular South Indian dish that originated in Mysore, Karnataka. It is a crispy and soft dosa that is spiced with red chutney and served with a potato dish, along with coconut chutney and sambar.

Chicken Curry

Chicken Curry is a traditional Indian dish made by simmering chicken pieces with plenty of spices, herbs, onions and tomatoes. It is believed to have originated in India & now made and enjoyed all over the world.

Malai Kofta

Malai Kofta is a very popular Indian vegetarian dish where balls (kofta) made of potato and paneer are deep fried and served with a creamy and spiced tomato based curry.

Idly & Vada

Idli and Vada (also known as Medu Vada) is a popular South Indian breakfast dish that is enjoyed across the country. It is made with a combination of lentils and rice, and is usually served with coconut chutney and sambar.
