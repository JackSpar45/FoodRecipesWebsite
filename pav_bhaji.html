Pav Bhaji Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • For Bhaji:
    • 2 cups mixed vegetables (potatoes, peas, carrots, beans, cauliflower), boiled and mashed
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 1 capsicum (bell pepper), finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1 tablespoon pav bhaji masala
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 4-5 cloves garlic, minced
    • A small piece of ginger, minced
    • Butter for cooking
    • Chopped cilantro for garnishing
    • Lemon wedges
    • Salt to taste
  • For Pav:
    • 6 pav buns
    • Butter for toasting

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. For Bhaji:
    • Heat butter in a pan. Add cumin seeds and mustard seeds.
    • Saut√© chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until onions turn translucent.
    • Add chopped capsicum and tomatoes. Cook until tomatoes soften.
    • Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
    • Add boiled and mashed vegetables. Mix and cook for a few minutes.
    • Mash the mixture using a masher until well combined and slightly chunky.
    • Garnish with chopped cilantro and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  2. For Pav:
    • Slit pav buns horizontally but not all the way through.
    • Spread butter on the inner sides and toast on a pan until golden brown.
  3. Serve the hot bhaji with toasted pav, chopped onions, and lemon wedges.
