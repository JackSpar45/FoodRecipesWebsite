Idli Vada Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • For Idli:
    • 2 cups idli rice
    • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
    • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    • Salt to taste
  • For Vada:
    • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
    • 1/4 cup rice flour
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns
    • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
    • A pinch of asafoetida
    • Curry leaves, chopped
    • Salt to taste
    • Oil for frying

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. For Idli:
    • Soak idli rice and fenugreek seeds together for 4-6 hours.
    • Soak urad dal separately for 4-6 hours.
    • Grind both rice and urad dal separately into smooth batters.
    • Mix the batters, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.
    • Pour the batter into idli molds and steam until cooked.
  2. For Vada:
    • Soak urad dal for 4-6 hours.
    • Grind into a thick and fluffy batter, adding minimal water.
    • Add rice flour, cumin seeds, black peppercorns, green chilies, asafoetida, curry leaves, and salt to the batter. Mix well.
    • Heat oil for frying.
    • Wet your hands, take a portion of batter, and shape it into a round vada. Make a hole in the center.
    • Gently slide the vada into hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy.
