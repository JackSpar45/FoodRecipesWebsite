Masala Dosa Recipe

INGREDIENTS

  • For Dosa Batter:
    • 2 cups rice
    • 1/2 cup urad dal (split black gram)
    • 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
    • Salt to taste
  • For Potato Masala:
    • 4-5 boiled potatoes, mashed
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1-2 green chilies, chopped
    • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • A pinch of asafoetida
    • Curry leaves
    • Oil for cooking
    • Salt to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. For Dosa Batter:
    • Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 4-6 hours.
    • Grind into a smooth batter using water. Ferment overnight.
    • Add salt to the batter before making dosas.
  2. For Potato Masala:
    • Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter.
    • Add cumin seeds, chopped onions, green chilies, curry leaves, and asafoetida. Saut√© until onions are translucent.
    • Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes. Mix well.
    • Add salt and cook for a few minutes. Keep the masala slightly chunky.
  3. To Make Dosas:
    • Heat a dosa tava (griddle) and grease it with oil.
    • Pour a ladleful of dosa batter in the center and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
    • Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
    • Place a portion of the potato masala on one half of the dosa.
    • Fold the other half over the masala to make a crescent shape.
